            reading and writing. These areas of proficiency form the basis of
            the learner aims, objectives and outcomes for the course.

            to repeat the level a maximum of once.
          </p>

          <p class="mb-30 mt-10 p-justify">

          </p>

            an institute’s placement test of grammar & reading, writing and
            speaking test. These tests and scales form the basis of the
            Placement Tests. Because the courses can accommodate students with
            only a very low grasp of English, Institute of Business and
            Management (Victoria) is able to accept students into the Elementary
            course even if they are at a very low level.

            <strong><i class="fa fa-check theme-color"></i></strong> Each week

            Vocabulary, Grammar and Reading. Student will be given mark for
            their speaking marking criteria on different level.
          </p>

          <p class="mt-10 mb-10 p-justify">
            <strong><i class="fa fa-check theme-color"></i></strong> Each week

            instructions for the teacher are in the Teacher’s book Students are
            marked according to the Formative Writing Marking Criteria which
            awards a score of A, B or C. The exceptions are in weeks 5 and 10
            when students take a summative writing task.
          </p>

          <p class="mt-10 mb-10 p-justify">

            Listening, Reading and Writing. The tests are completed over 2
            hours.
          </p>

          <p class="mt-10 mb-10 p-justify">
